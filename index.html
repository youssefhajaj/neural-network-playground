The Math

Forward Pass

$$z^{[l]} = W^{[l]}a^{[l-1]} + b^{[l]}$$
$$a^{[l]} = g(z^{[l]})$$

Backpropagation

$$\delta^{[L]} = \nabla_a \mathcal{L} \odot g'(z^{[L]})$$
$$\delta^{[l]} = (W^{[l+1]})^T \delta^{[l+1]} \odot g'(z^{[l]})$$

Gradient Descent

$$W^{[l]} = W^{[l]} - \alpha \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial W^{[l]}}$$
